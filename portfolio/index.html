Personal Projects

Puppeteering

Puppeteering is a QWOP-esque game made during the RIT Game Dev Club Day of the Dead Jam where you guide a puppet through a maze filled with traps. The only animations implemented are the hand motions by design, with the rest of the game controlled entirely through Unity's physics engine

Strength

Strength is a dungeon-crawling beat-em-up made during the ROC Game Dev Wild Magic Game Jam. By switching between two forms during combat, you're forced to use two different resources that feed into one another when used, requiring the player to actively switch between different styles of play
