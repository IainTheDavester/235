Popcorn Boy

Proposal Project About

The most innovative shooter of our time...

...is called Superhot, and is not this game at all. This is Popcorn Boy, an action platform dealing with the most dangerous foes of all - customers. Hungry, bored, pissed-off customers. As an usher, it's your sacred duty to annihilate serve these customers to the best of your ability. You're being paid minimum wage however, so the best of your ability entails sloppily cleaning up messes and strategically ignoring customer complaints. Have fun!
